PromotionSure

PromotionSure is an online examination-practice application for Nigerian public servants preparing for public service promotion examinations.

Candidates can practise objective questions and oral responses by module, complete timed practice sets, and review their scores and answers.

If a candidate chooses Google sign-in, PromotionSure uses their Google account name and email only to create or access their PromotionSure account.
